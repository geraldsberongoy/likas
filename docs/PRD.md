# **LIKAS**

*Your **companion** when calamity strikes the nation*

*Likas, in Filipino, means nature — yet it also means to evacuate. An AI-powered platform that helps citizens evacuate during disasters caused by nature.*


## OVERVIEW

**For** Filipino communities in disaster-prone areas who face annual typhoons, earthquakes, and volcanic eruptions, **who** lose access to critical survival information when communication fails during emergencies, **our** LIKAS mobile application **is** an offline AI-powered disaster companion **that** provides personalized evacuation routes, emergency protocols, and life-saving guidance — all accessible without internet when disaster strikes.

## CHALLENGE STATEMENT

When typhoons strike and cell towers fail, communities lose access to life-saving information when they need it most. This happened during the Philippines' 2024 typhoon season, when six massive storms hit within just 30 days—something weather experts called "never seen before."¹ These disasters affected over 15 million people and overwhelmed government systems that could not keep up.² The crisis showed a major problem: when phones, internet, and radio fail during disasters, people are left without information about where to go or how to stay safe.³

The Philippines faces this multi-disaster threat every year. The country gets hit by about 20 typhoons annually, experiences 100-150 earthquakes strong enough to feel, and has active volcanoes that threaten nearby communities.⁴,⁵ Recent examples include Taal Volcano's December 2024 eruption that sent ash clouds 2,800 meters high, and Mayon Volcano's 2023 eruption that displaced over 38,000 people.⁶,⁷ The 2024 typhoon season alone caused over ₱21 billion in damage, while data from 2010-2019 shows natural disasters killed over 12,000 Filipinos and caused ₱463 billion in total damage.⁸,⁹ With 74% of the population living in disaster-prone areas, there is an urgent need for disaster preparation tools that work without internet or phone service during emergencies.¹⁰

## SOLUTION

LIKAS is an **offline-first AI-powered disaster companion** designed for Filipino communities in disaster-prone areas. It transforms your smartphone into a vital survival tool, ensuring you have critical information even when cell towers are down and the internet is out. Our solution leverages pre-loaded data and intelligent on-device AI processing to provide immediate and context-driven guidance without any external connection. This directly addresses the critical gap in disaster response, empowering individuals to act decisively when government systems are overwhelmed.

### FEATURES

When typhoons rage, earthquakes shake, or volcanoes erupt, communication often fails. News reports from outlets regularly show how entire areas become cut off, leaving people without vital information. This happened recently in the 2024 typhoon season, when 15 million Filipinos lost access to critical updates. LIKAS solves this by working completely offline.

### **How LIKAS Keeps You Safe:**

**1\. Go-To Evacuation Routes (Typhoon & Flood):**

* **Problem:** When floods rise in places like Cabanatuan City, or a typhoon hits, knowing the safest way out becomes impossible as signals drop.  
* **Solution:** LIKAS gives you instant, clear routes to the nearest evacuation centers. It uses offline maps with trusted data, consistent with **PAGASA** advisories, so you always know where to go, even when there's no internet. You can easily see how far and how long it will take to get to safety.

**2\. Volcanic Emergency Guide:**

* **Problem:** Living near active volcanoes means sudden eruptions can occur, leaving communities unsure of what to do.  
* **Solution:** LIKAS provides step-by-step instructions for before, during, and after a volcanic eruption. This critical information, based on **PHIVOLCS** guidelines, is always on your phone. You'll know exactly how to prepare, react to ashfall, and stay safe after the eruption, with clear visuals to guide you.

**3\. Earthquake Survival Plan:**

* **Problem:** Earthquakes strike without warning, often causing panic and confusion when communication is down.  
* **Solution:** LIKAS teaches you crucial earthquake survival skills. You'll learn the "Drop, Cover, Hold" technique and access an offline map showing active fault lines from **PHIVOLCS**. This empowers you to react quickly and protect yourself during unpredictable tremors.

**4\. Always-On AI Assistant:**

* **Problem:** During disasters, quick and accurate information is vital, but traditional communication often fails.  
* **Solution:** Our special AI works directly on your phone, without needing internet. It quickly understands your needs and gives you the right information instantly. This ensures LIKAS is always reliable, a true companion for you and your family, fulfilling **NDRRMC**'s goal of strong community preparedness, even when the power is out and networks are down.
