# Mascot Generation Prompt: Likas App

This document serves as the prompt input for an AI image/character generation service to create the official mascot for the **Likas** application.

---

## App Context: Likas
**Likas** is a disaster preparedness and navigation application focused on helping users in the Philippines find safe routes, identify evacuation centers, and access life-saving information during emergencies. The brand tone is **trustworthy, heroic, calm, modern, and accessible.**

## Mascot Design Requirements: "Likas" (The Philippine Eagle)

### Core Inspiration
The mascot is a **Philippine Eagle**—the national bird of the Philippines. It should be animated/styled in a friendly, approachable, yet capable manner, suitable for an app interface.

### Visual Style
- **Animation/Style:** 2D or 3D vector-style, "Pixar-esque" or high-quality mobile app mascot style (clean lines, friendly eyes, warm lighting).
- **Colors:** Incorporate the natural colors of the Philippine Eagle (browns, creams, dark accents) but use a vibrant **primary green** (hex: `#22c55e` or similar) as the hero accent color (perhaps a scarf, a small bag, or the design background) to tie into the Likas app branding.
- **Personality:** Courageous, watchful, intelligent, and helpful. It should look like an ally during a crisis.
- **Pose:** Friendly, standing or flying, possibly pointing to a map, holding a first-aid kit, or offering a reassuring gesture.

## Prompt Construction
Use the following structured prompt when interacting with the Gemini API:

> "Create a high-quality, 3D-animated mascot character of a Philippine Eagle. 
> 
> - **Visuals:** The eagle should be friendly, heroic, and approachable, with a clean and modern design. It should be inspired by the natural look of a Philippine Eagle (brown/cream plumage) but stylized for a mobile app. 
> - **Accents:** Incorporate a vibrant 'Likas' signature green (a deep, reliable forest green) into the design—perhaps as a small, functional accessory like a messenger scarf or a lightweight gear harness. 
> - **Personality:** The expression should be watchful, smart, and reassuring. 
> - **Context:** The mascot is the guide for a disaster preparedness app called 'Likas' in the Philippines. It should embody safety and readiness. 
> - **Style:** Clean, professional 3D vector style, vibrant, high-quality, friendly, suitable for use in an app UI."

---
## Suggested Use Cases for the Mascot
- **Onboarding:** Welcoming the user to the app.
- **Navigation:** Appearing near the route information.
- **Alerts:** Expressing caution or safety messages.
- **Chat:** The primary avatar for the Likas AI assistant.
